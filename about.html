What is ByteWise?

ByteWise is a personal tech blog covering AI tools, gadgets, coding, productivity, and the ideas shaping how we work — written in plain English, without the jargon or hype.

Most tech content is either too shallow ("here are 10 AI tools!") or too technical for anyone outside a specific field. ByteWise tries to be the middle ground: detailed enough to be genuinely useful, but written so anyone curious can follow along.

Why Read This?

There's no paywall. No sponsored rankings. No affiliate-driven "best of" lists where the #1 product is the one that paid the most. Just honest takes on what's actually worth your time and money.

Everything is written from Tamil Nadu, India — so prices, availability, and context are relevant to Indian readers, even when covering global tools.

How to Follow

The best way to keep up is to subscribe to the newsletter — new articles go out whenever something worth writing about happens. No fixed schedule, no daily noise.
